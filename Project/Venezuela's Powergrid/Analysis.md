#Analysis of OSRS prices on hot commodities following Venezuela's Power Grid bombing


On january 3, 2026 the US bombed Venezuela's power grid when
they kidnapped their president for extradition to the
US for charge of Drug Conspiracy. It's been hypothesized
that the majority of bot accounts on runescape are located
inside of Venezula due to the country's extreme inflation. Because 
the price of OSRS in game gold to US dollars offers a 
tempting monetary conversion that is quite profitable in countries
where their local currency is inflated. Venezuelan run bot farms have
hundreds of characters that "farm" low value items that allow
a high trade volumes to turn profits on items most people don't wish
to do since the task is considered menial. As such, after the bombing
of Venezuel's powergrid, it was believed that the prices of hundreds
of necessary commodities critical for average players to efficiently
play end game content skyrocketed. This is believed to have last for
several days until their power grid became more stable. As such, we 
are looking at several importantly traded items to analysis whether
such claims are true and identifiable\
